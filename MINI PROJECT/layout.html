Water Pipeline System

Data Points to Track

The following metrics will be tracked in the water management system:

Water Flow

Measure flow rates at different pipeline points

Tank Levels

Monitor water levels in both main tanks

Room Consumption

Track individual room water usage

Pressure Levels

Monitor water pressure in the system

Temperature

Track water temperature variations

Usage Patterns

Identify peak usage times and trends
